ABOUT ME

Hey! My name is Nathaniel Qin. I'm currently an undergraduate junior at UC San Diego studying Human Computer Interaction with a minor in Computer Science. I'm currently seeking an internship for the summer of 2019!

Human-centered design is a principle that I believe strongly in. Nothing can be designed well without understanding the needs of the userbase. As Don Norman says, the goal is to help the user remember the pleasantries of their interaction, and this can only be accomplished through a well-developed, honed user experience.

With my background in both design and development, I've come to appreciate both roles. Without strong design, the development lacks a solid foundation. Without efficient development, the design never comes to fruition. Both are mutually dependent!

In my free time, I love to relax by playing the piano, reading a good book, or going on hikes and new adventures.
