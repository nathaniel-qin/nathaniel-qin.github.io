Swap

MOBILE WEB APP

LA Hacks 2019

April 2019

AT A GLANCE

Swap is a mobile web application designed mainly to help foster community. The premise is similar to Tinder, in which users upload photos of things that they are willing to trade away. Other users can then swipe left or right on items they're interested in, which brings them into a direct message to further discuss the details of their trade. The trading is not limited to physical items, however, and can easily be expanded for users to share hobbies, cuisine, cultures, or even knowledge.

My Collaborators: Edward Chen, Miranda Go, Christopher Maytom
