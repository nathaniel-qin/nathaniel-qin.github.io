AT A GLANCE

As the carpool coordinator for my college fellowship, I created Carpool as a way to make my job a little easier. It's a web application that allows passengers to request rides. Most of the work is still manual, since the data simply gets inputted into a spreadsheet for my easy access and sorting. There is also a weekly puzzle for those who want to attempt it.
