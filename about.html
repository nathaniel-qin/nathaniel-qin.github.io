Welcome! I'm glad you stopped by.

I'm currently a third-year student at UC San Diego studying for a B.S. in Human Computer Interaction and a minor in Computer Science. This summer, I'll be joining IBM in Littleton, MA as a Software Design Intern, but I'm always open to discuss future opportunities!

I'm originally from the San Francisco Bay Area, so being that I attend school in La Jolla, I like to take advantage of the free time I have as a student to explore, whether to try new food, tackle scenic hikes, or even meet new people. Feel free to reach out so we can get to know each other better! My contact info is in my resume and my social links are in the footer.
